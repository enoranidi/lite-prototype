Check if you need an export licence and apply for a licence online

If your business is considering exporting items, goods or services such as consultancy, you will need to check if you need a licence to export from the UK.

You may need a licence even if you are only exporting goods temporarily eg: taking a sample to an exhibition.

If you do need an export licence, you will get the option to apply for one by using this service.
! Warning Exporting goods without the right licence is a criminal offence, so it's important to check first.
