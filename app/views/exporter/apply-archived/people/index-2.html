What does this person do?

 This person is the:
The person sending the goods. You must include information about the exporter if you are not the exporter, for example if you are an agent.
The person using your goods (incorporating them into an end product). They will either:
  • use the goods as they are, or
  • sell the goods as they are, or
  • put the goods into another product and then use them /sell them in that other product
The person who is involved in negotiating the export deal.
The person who looks after the goods temporarily, on a stopover.

This is anyone who accepts the goods after their first stop, on the way to their final destination.
The person who does the final review and then submits the paperwork for this export.
The person who gives advice about this export.
This could be anyone else involved in the export process. For example, this could be the person you speak to if your goods get held up at customs.
